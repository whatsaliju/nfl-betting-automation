# Ethical and Legal Declarations
## WARPS-NFL: A Preseason Win-Total Forecasting Model for the National Football League

---

**Conflict of Interest Statement**

The author declares no conflicts of interest. This research was conducted independently with no financial relationships with any organizations that could be perceived to have influenced the study.

---

**Funding Statement**

This research received no external funding. All work was conducted independently by the author.

---

**Data Availability Statement**

All data used in this study are publicly available. Play-by-play data are sourced from the nflfastR R package (Baldwin and Carl, 2020), available at https://github.com/nflverse/nflfastR. Historical preseason win totals are sourced from nflverse (https://github.com/nflverse/nfldata). All analysis code and output files are available at https://github.com/whatsaliju/nfl-betting-automation under the MIT License.

Not applicable. This study uses only publicly available, aggregated sports statistics. No human subjects, patients, clinical data, or personally identifiable information were involved in this research.
